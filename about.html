Our Mission

Si loo bixiyo waxbarasho tayo leh oo kobcisa hal-abuurka, fikirka muhiimka ah, iyo waxbarashada nolosha oo dhan si badbaado leh, loo wada dhan yahay, oo ku haboon loo diyaariyay.

Our Vision

Si aan u noqono machad waxbarasho oo hormuud ah oo awood u siinaya ardayda si ay u gaadhaan awoodooda buuxda, bulshadana si togan wax ugu biiriyaan.
